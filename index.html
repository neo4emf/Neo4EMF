Big Models made possible

Fast Storage, On-demand Loading and Unloading of your large scale EMF Models

Neo4EMF provides a No-SQL database persistence framework based on Neo4j, which is a transactional property-graph database that proved a remarkable running speed for connected data operations compared to relational databases.

What makes Neo4EMF different is the use of a sophisticated unloading approach apart from simple Soft/Weak references
