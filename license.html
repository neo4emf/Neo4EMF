Licensing notes

NeoEMF uses the Neo4J graph database as its persistence back-end. Neo4J is free software licensed under the terms of the GNU General Public License, and as such, cannot be redistributed with EPL licensed software.

We are working on a solution to easily help users to download Neo4j if needed (NeoEMF works without Neo4j, using the default Blueprints implementation or MapDB).
